Our Menu

Dollar's menu is carefully curated to bring customers a selection of flavors and textures to satisfy different tastes and cravings.

Our Story

Dollar was founded in 2010 by Chef, Lionel Messi, as a celebration of the doughnut. Inspired by his Argentian heritage as well as European influences, Fany set out to craft an iconic version of this classic treat.

After months of work in the kitchen perfecting her recipe, Fany now provides the world with a unique, refined doughnut that strikes the perfect balance of flavor and texture.
